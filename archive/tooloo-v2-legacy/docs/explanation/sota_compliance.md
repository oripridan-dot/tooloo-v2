# SOTA Compliance & Governance

TooLoo V2 adheres to State-Of-The-Art (SOTA) practices and incorporates governance measures, particularly concerning LLM-generated content.

## Documentation Structure (Diátaxis)

Following the Diátaxis framework, documentation is categorized into:

*   **Tutorials**: Step-by-step guides for learning TooLoo V2 (e.g., `docs/tutorials/getting_started.md`).
*   **How-Tos**: Solutions to specific problems (e.g., `docs/how-to/define_dag.md`).
*   **Reference**: Detailed descriptions of components and APIs (e.g., `docs/reference/config.py.md`).
*   **Explanation**: Conceptual overviews and architectural details (e.g., `docs/explanation/pipeline_architecture.md`).

## Diagrams (Mermaid.js)

Inline diagrams are generated using Mermaid.js syntax, ensuring broad compatibility across platforms like GitHub, Notion, and Confluence.

## LLM-Generated Runbooks & Compliance

*   **SOX & ISO 27001**: To ensure compliance with regulations such as the Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX) and ISO 27001 standards for information security, any runbooks, operational procedures, or critical configurations generated by LLMs *must* undergo mandatory human review before being used in operational environments.
*   **Traceability**: Logging and tracing (via OpenTelemetry) provide an audit trail for actions performed within the TooLoo V2 system.

This dual approach of leveraging SOTA technology while maintaining rigorous human oversight ensures both innovation and operational integrity.
